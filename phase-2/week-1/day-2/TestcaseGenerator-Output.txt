Test Case ID: TC_IP_001
Title: Verify Successful Policy Purchase

Preconditions:
- User is logged in with an active account.

Test Steps:
1. Navigate to the Policy Purchase page.
2. Select a policy type.
3. Enter valid customer details.
4. Pay the premium amount.
5. Confirm the purchase.

Expected Result:
- The policy is successfully purchased.
- A purchase confirmation message is displayed.
